Scoring rules:
At the beginning, each player receives 1200 points.
Every second spent on solving a question subtracts 10 points.
In addition, after guessing the name of the capital, for each uncovered letter you get an additional 100 points.
For every other life you receive an additional 200 points.

Lifes:
You start the game with 5 lives.
You lose a life for a wrongly guessed letter. If you guess the city's name wrong, you lose 2 lives.
